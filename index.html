About Me
Born and raised in San Diego, California. Jannah graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a major in Pharmaceutical Sciences and a minor in Economics. However, when it was time to head to pharmacy school, she declined and experienced what some would call a quarter-life crisis. For the past three years, Jannah has worked as a Native English Teacher and Lecturer in Seoul, South Korea and Tokyo, Japan. While abroad, she realized the importance of web development in the everyday lives of people around the world, whether it be through social settings managing clinical information, or even keeping track of student data. Since then, Jannah has been working as technical support at a innovative insulin pump company, but aspires to learn more about the coding world.
